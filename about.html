s/buy/make/ is a blog about making things, mostly out of data and code.

The name comes from the syntax of the sed command line utility, in words it reads "replace instances of the word 'buy' with the word 'make'". This is how I want to spend my time -- at the end of the day it's more fun and more rewarding to create something new than to consume something that someone else made.
